Learn more about TasteSync and how it works.

TasteSync is a food and drink pairing app that helps users discover drinks that go well with their meals. It gives pairing suggestions, explains why they work, and lets users save favorites in a drink diary.

You can enter a meal and receive suggested drink pairings. As the app grows, personalization can be improved using your drink diary, saved ratings, and future preference settings.
