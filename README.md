# TasteSync: A Food and Drink Pairing Recommendation System

A lot of people don’t really know what drink goes best with their food. Sometimes you order something that sounds good, but once you take a sip with your meal, 
it just doesn’t match and kind of ruins the taste. This can happen when eating at home, at restaurants, or even during special occasions like business dinners.
Right now, people either guess, search online, or rely on experience when choosing a drink. There isn’t a simple tool where you can just type in your meal and 
get helpful suggestions right away. Our project aims to create a system that recommends drinks based on the food someone is eating. Instead of guessing, users 
will get suggestions that explain why a drink pairs well with their meal. This could be helpful for casual dining, date nights, or even professional settings 
where choosing the right drink matters.
